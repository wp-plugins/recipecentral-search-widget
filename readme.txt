Add a widget to your site to search for recipes on your RecipeCentral.com site.

== Description ==

This plugin will give you a widget on your site to search recipes on RecipeCentral.com.

Recipe Central is a turnkey program that blends the power of your digital brand with our quality-tested recipe database. In just a few simple steps, our white-labeled recipe and video content becomes a custom-branded recipe user experience on your website.


== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

e.g.

1. Upload `recipecentral.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Go to Appearance -> Widget and enter your RecipeCentral.com subdomain.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Will the visitor using the widget leave my site? =

Yes. The widget will submit the search to your subdomain on RecipeCentral.com
